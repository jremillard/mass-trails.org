Welcome

This website has free trail maps for 5,500 miles of trails on over 15,000 conservation properties and parks in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.

Trail maps can be found by navigating on the overall map, searching by town, or searching by landowner. Please jump in and explore!

Statewide Trail Map ⋅ Town List ⋅ Land Owners List

If you know what you are looking for, use the search page.

OpenStreetMap powers mass-trails.org allowing the trail maps to be 100% free: no subscription, no user accounts, no advertisements, no registrations, no required phone app, and no in-app purchases.

Many conservation commissions and conservation groups need maps of their properties and trails. Mass-trails can help provide a solution. Please see the contributing page for more details.
